भरोसेमंद काम — How we keep RealHands safe and honest.

🆓

100% free — no commission

Workers and employers pay nothing to RealHands. Wages go directly from the employer to the worker — we never take a cut.

✅

Verified workers

Every worker confirms their phone number, and we encourage a photo and ID check. Verified workers carry a ✔️ badge so employers know they're real.

⭐

Ratings & reviews

After each job, employers rate the worker. Honest ratings and reviews build a worker's reputation over time — so good work is rewarded.

🔁

Replacement guarantee

If a confirmed worker doesn't show up, message us and we'll help you find a replacement — so your work never stops.

📞

Real human support

A real person is one WhatsApp message away. Any problem — payment, no-show, or a complaint — we help resolve it.

🔒

Your number stays private

Phone numbers are shared only when a worker and employer agree to connect for a specific job.
