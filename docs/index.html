Is it really free? / क्या यह मुफ़्त है?
Yes — 100% free for both workers and employers. No charges, no commission.
Do I need to download an app?
No. Everything works on WhatsApp and this website. You can also "Add to Home Screen" to use it like an app.
How do I register as a worker?
Tap "Find Work", fill the quick form (name, trade, area, rate), and you're listed. Employers contact you on WhatsApp.
How fast can I find workers?
Usually within an hour for common trades in Hyderabad. We connect you with available workers nearby.
Which areas do you cover?
Currently Hyderabad. Bangalore, Chennai & Delhi are coming soon.
